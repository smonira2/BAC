Bangladesh Accreditation Council



know more

about

The Jatiya Sangsad passed the Bangladesh Accreditation Council Bill 2017 aiming to ensure quality of higher education at both public and private universities. University Grants Commission officials and owners of private university said that the accreditation council would be helpful to maintain quality education at all the universities specially to the private universities which are facing question over quality. Education minister Nurul Islam Nahid while moving the bill said that the council would helpful for the extensive monitoring of academic activities of the universities in the long run higher educations. Later, the house with speaker Shirin Sharmin Chaudhury in the chair passed the bill by voice vote. As per the bill, the council would be autonomous statutory body headed by a chairman with four full-time and eight part-time members.The government has moved to form an accreditation council to ensure the standard of higher education, around one and half years after the enactment of the law.
